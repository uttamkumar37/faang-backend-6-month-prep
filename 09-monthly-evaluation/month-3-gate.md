## Pass/Fail Criteria
Pass: 75+ overall, production debugging at least 6/10, system design at least 11/15. Fail: cannot debug slow API or Kafka lag at checklist level.

## Recovery Plan If Low
Spend one week on debugging playbooks, add observability to project, and run one incident mock.
