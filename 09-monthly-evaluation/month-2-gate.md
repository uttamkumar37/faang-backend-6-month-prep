## Pass/Fail Criteria
Pass: 75+ overall, Java/backend at least 11/15, LLD at least 6/10. Fail: cannot explain JVM memory or transaction basics.

## Recovery Plan If Low
Run a JVM/concurrency repair week, implement two LLD skeletons, and redo SQL/indexing drills.

## Interview Questions
