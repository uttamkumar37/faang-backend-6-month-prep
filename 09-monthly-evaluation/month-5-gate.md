## Pass/Fail Criteria
Pass: 80+ overall, mock average pass or higher, company plan complete. Fail: behavioral stories not ready or mocks still borderline.

## Recovery Plan If Low
Reduce new content, run 4 targeted mocks, repair the top 3 repeated issues, and map stories to target companies.
